Enhancements / New Features

  • Better GPS Checking – The app now checks your location signal every few seconds to make sure it’s accurate.
  • GPS Reminder Popup – Before entering the main menu, the app will remind you to turn on location. If it’s off, the app will close automatically to avoid errors.
  • GPS Signal Indicator – See your GPS signal strength (Good or Weak) at the top of the main menu.
  • No Need to Restart for Location – You don’t have to close and reopen the app to refresh your location anymore.
  • Stuck Picture Problem – Fixed an issue where the first photo was reused even after taking a new one.
  • Better Camera Compatibility – The camera now works properly on all Android versions, especially newer ones with stricter permissions.
  • Logs – After capturing a photo, the app will automatically go to the logs so you can confirm it was saved successfully.
  • View Photos in Gallery – Open captured photos directly in your phone’s gallery from the logs.
  • Automatic Employee Info Update – If we update your name, department, or other info in the company database, the app will update it automatically.
  • No More Duplicate Uploads – Each log is uploaded only once to prevent double entries.
  • Crash Fixes – Fixed issues that caused the app to crash when reconnecting to the internet.
